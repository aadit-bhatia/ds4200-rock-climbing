My Climbing Experiences

Over the past two years, rock climbing has become an integral part of my lifestyle. I've explored various climbing styles, including outdoor bouldering, gym, and top-rope. Each style offers unique challenges and rewards. Bouldering has honed my problem-solving skills, focusing intensely on short, powerful routes. Top-rope climbing provided a safe environment for improving endurance and technique. Outdoors climbing, however, has been the most rewarding, offering a sense of freedom and community with my friends, who work to support and encourage each other to better themselves.

Favorite Gyms and Crags:

  • Rock Spot Brookline
  • CRG Fenway
  • Hammond Pond, Brookline
  • Lincoln Woods, Lincoln
